Calculator Information

  1. Superannuation Guarantee Contribution is 9.5% of Gross Salary.
  2. Contribution tax rate is 15%. For taxable income above $300,000 the contributions tax rate increases to 30%.
  3. This model has a 'wage increase rate' which is currently set to the same value as inflation, 3.5% per annum. Salary sacrifice and personal contributions are indexed to inflation.
  4. No deductions made for insurance premiums.
  5. The assumed earnings rate for all funds, as used in this calculation, is 6% before fees and taxes.
  6. The tax on earnings in all funds is calculated at 15%.
  7. Assume that the establishment fee is zero
  8. Assume that there is no adviser service fee.
  9. This is a model not a prediction. Results are only estimates, the actual amounts may be higher or lower. We cannot predict things that will affect your decision such as movements in investment markets.
  10. This calculator is not intended to be your sole source of information when making a financial decision. You should consider seeking advice from a qualified financial advisor.
  11. This calculator does not work for people in defined benefit funds.
  12. The superannuation amounts are shown in todayâ€™s dollars.
  13. This is a fee comparison calculator, hence we assume that the investment earnings, insurance premiums and all other factors are the same between a low cost fund and your fund.
  14. Real investment earnings are 3% per year after inflation.
